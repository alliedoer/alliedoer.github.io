Party Favor

Billie Eilish

party favor” is the fifth song on Billie Eilish’s debut EP, don’t smile at me. The song is formatted as a voicemail that Billie is leaving for a boy who has become possessive of her and is an attempt at trying to force him out of her life. The track was included as the A-Side of the exclusive 7" inch vinyl for Record Store Day 2018, along with her studio cover of Drake’s“Hotline Bling” as the B-Side.
