7/27

Fifth Harmony

7/27 is the second studio album by American girl group Fifth Harmony[note 1], released by Syco Music and Epic Records on May 27, 2016. The record is the follow-up to the group's debut studio album Reflection. Its lyrics discuss themes of female empowermentand love. It features guest appearances by American R&B and hip hop artists Ty Dolla Sign, Fetty Wap and Missy Elliott, and collaborations with producers Jack Antonoff, Kygo and Norwegian duo Stargate. 7/27 is primarily a pop, tropical house and R&B record that includes elements of reggae, funk, electronic dance music, hip hop and trap. Unlike the genres explored on Reflection, 7/27'ssongs incorporate new genres like tropical house. This is the final Fifth Harmony album to feature Camila Cabello, who left the group in December 2016 to pursue a solo career.
